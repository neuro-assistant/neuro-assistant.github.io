Neuro Assistant

A collection of tools for healthcare professionals working with neurological and neurosurgical patients.

Created by clinicians for clinicians.

Learn more Â»

Comprehensive

  • A collection of calculators, grading systems and scales commonly used in neurological and neurosurgical clinical practice.
  • Localization aids for vascular lesions, cranial and peripheral nerves, including visual guides and textual reference.
  • Selected short clinical and drug references with the most essential and clinically relevant information in various aspects of practice.

Usable

The content is relevant and discoverable and the user interface intuitive and fast. All content is searchable by tags or full-text queries within the app or from the mobile device's search function.

Up-to-date

All information is provided after review of the most prominent and up-to-date literature. Links to any original articles or other relevant resources are provided in all content and tools.
